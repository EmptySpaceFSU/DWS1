Equations that Changed History

An article by: Christopher Ayers

"Equations are a vital part of our culture. The stories behind them --- the people who
discovered/invented them and the periods in which they lived --- are fascinating." - Ian Steward

The List

  1. The Pythagorean Theorem

    • What does it mean: The square of the hypotenuse of a triangle is equal to the sum of the squares of its legs.
    • What does it look like: A2+B2=C2

  2. The origin of complex numbers

    • What does it mean: The square of an imaginary number is negative.
    • What does it look like: i2 = -1

  3. Second law of thermodynamics

    • What does it mean?: Energy and heat dissipate over time.
    • What does it look like: dS >= 0

  4. Einstein's theory of relativity

    • What does it mean?: Energy equals mass times the speed of light squared.
    • What does it look like: E=mc2

  5. Shannon's information theory

    • What does it mean?: Estimates the amount of data in a piece of code by the probabilities of its component symbols.
    • What does it look like? H=-Σp(x)logp(x)
